## A Data-Driven Academic Website
Like many modern academic websites, this site separates **content** from **presentation**:

- Research content is written in structured **Markdown** and **YAML**
- Layout and styling are handled by reusable templates
- Updates are automatically published when changes are committed to GitHub

This approach provides:
- Full control over content
- No dependence on proprietary platforms
- Easy recovery and long-term maintainability
- Seamless integration with code, data, and reproducible research workflows
